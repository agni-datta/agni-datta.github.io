About

I am a graduate student completing my master's in computer science, with a minor in cybersecurity, at VIT Bhopal University in India. I did my master's thesis under the mentorship of Matteo Campanelli. This summer, I'm at CISPA as a research assistant, hosted by Doreen Riepel.

Research Interests

My research lies in theoretical computer science, focusing on the foundations of cryptography and its interface with neighboring areas. I study proof systems and advanced cryptographic primitives, including non-interactive zero-knowledge proofs and indistinguishability obfuscation. I am also beginning to explore quantum-native cryptography.

Research areas:

  • Foundations of Cryptography
  • Quantum Cryptography
  • Probabilistic Proofs
  • Property Testing
